## 1. Executive Summary
This document outlines the **3-Tab Executive Sales Story** designed in Tableau. The goal is to provide a structured narrative flow for the retail leadership team, guiding them from high-level corporate KPIs down to operational supply chain performance and regional opportunities.

---

## 2. Story Flow & Narrative Structure

### Tab 1: Executive Overview (The "What")
*   **Story Point:** "Gross sales reached **$217.0M** with a healthy **15.35%** profit margin, driven primarily by Q4 seasonal demand."
*   **Focus:** Monitors high-level indicators (Sales, Profit, AOV, Margin) and segment share.
*   **Performance:** Consumer segment dominates with **51.8%** sales volume.

### Tab 2: Operations & Logistics (The "Why")
*   **Story Point:** "Fulfillment bottlenecks in same-day shipping and high return rates in the Furniture category are eroding profitability."
*   **Focus:** Explores delivery SLAs, discount impact on profitability, and category return flags.
*   **Insights:** Same-day shipping averages **1.49 days**, failing to meet SLAs.

### Tab 3: Geographic Performance (The "Where")
*   **Story Point:** "Expansion opportunities lie in the West region, while the South requires targeted marketing support."
*   **Focus:** State-level maps and top 10 states sales horizontal bar charts.
*   **Insights:** California and Washington represent the highest gross sales states.

---

## 3. Dashboard Limitations & Next Analysis Steps
*   **Limitations:** The current data source does not contain customer acquisition cost (CAC) or inventory stockout frequencies.
*   **Suggested Next Analysis:** Connect the dashboard to Google Ads and warehouse inventory tables to evaluate advertising ROI and stockout costs.
